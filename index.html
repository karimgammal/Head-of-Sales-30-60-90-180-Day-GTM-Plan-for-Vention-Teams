Executive Summary

The Challenge

Vention Teams has a talented inherited sales team operating at 50% quota attainment. The opportunity is clear: transform this foundation into a high-performing, scalable revenue engine while building the systems, processes, and culture needed for sustainable growth.

The Solution

A comprehensive 6-month plan to: (1) Establish GTM foundations and lift current team performance, (2) Build repeatable processes and hire strategically, (3) Scale proven patterns and create a self-sustaining revenue machine, and (4) Achieve predictable MRR with clear career paths and team culture.
